## Overview

Wejha is a mobile application designed to help **high school students choose their academic path**.

Many students struggle to understand available study programs and career opportunities.  
Wejha provides guidance through tests, information, and interactive tools.

## Technologies

- Flutter
- Dart
- Chatbot Integration
- UX/UI Design

## Core Features

### Orientation Tests

Students can take tests that evaluate:

- interests
- strengths
- preferences

The results suggest suitable study paths.

### School & Program Explorer

Students can browse available programs and institutions with filters such as:

- field of study
- location
- required level

### Chatbot Assistance

A built-in chatbot helps users by answering questions such as:

- program information
- career guidance
- admission requirements

### Smart Filtering

Dynamic filters allow users to quickly explore educational opportunities based on their profile.

## Design

The application was designed with a focus on:

- simple navigation
- clear information hierarchy
- student-friendly UI

## Vision

Wejha aims to simplify the orientation process and empower students to make informed academic decisions.
